July 12th

Grey sky but no rain

Ran into Cuomo on my the next day back in town… God, They haven't changed a bit—same face, probably the same rotten personality too. But now they're selling ice cream in the park? (How did they even recognize me from that distance, waving like an idiot? As if that thing never happened.)

Their ice cream truck plays the most childish tune—fitting, really. I'd lose my mind surrounded by screaming kids all day, so I'll give them credit for that. Just that, though.

…The ice cream is good, actually. Might try to grab the chocolate flavor before the kids swarm next time.

What am I even writing? I'm not getting involved with Cuomo again.

- Noah·Everly
