July 11th

sunny

The old house remains unchanged from my childhood memories, save for the new neighbors (who talk far too loudly - I'll have to file a noise complaint if they carry on after dark). Years of city living with its constant car horns and work stress have left my nerves rather frayed.

That familiar musty smell greeted me when I opened the door. The furniture seems to have held up well enough, though everything's covered in a thick layer of dust. I'll need to pick up some proper cleaning supplies in town tomorrow.

Mother and father's bedroom stopped me short. All their things exactly as they left them. I hadn't expected the lump in my throat. Perhaps I'll keep most of it as it was - no need for drastic changes when the place already feels like home.

The back garden's become completely overgrown. Should really clear out those weeds and plant some proper flowers. Might be nice to have tea out there on a sunny afternoon.

- Noah·Everly
